About Me

I'm a self-taught Security Engineer with a practical, project-driven focus on solving complex security challenges.

Driven by a passion for technology, I pivoted my career to cybersecurity and dedicated myself to building the solutions I found most interesting. My "builder's mindset" is my core strength.

I thrive on solving puzzles and building robust, intelligent systems from the ground up. I am now seeking my first formal role where I can apply my engineering skills, my passion for AI-driven defense, and my relentless drive to help secure a forward-thinking organization.
